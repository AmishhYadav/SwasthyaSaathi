## About Jan Aushadhi Kendras
Jan Aushadhi Kendras are stores established under the Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Jan Aushadhi Pariyojana (PMBJP).
They sell quality generic medicines at affordable prices, often 50-90% cheaper than branded equivalents.
Over 10,000 Jan Aushadhi Kendras are operational across India.
Product range: 1800+ medicines and 300+ surgical/medical devices.
All medicines are tested for quality by NABL-accredited laboratories.
To find the nearest Jan Aushadhi Kendra, visit: http://janaushadhi.gov.in or call 1800-180-8080 (toll-free).
